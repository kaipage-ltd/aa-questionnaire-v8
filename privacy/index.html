Privacy Notice

How we use your diagnostic data.

This notice explains how Atelier + Avenue uses personal data when you complete the AI Readiness Diagnostic and request your profile, reveal page and PDF.

Version 2026-06-04 | Last updated 4 June 2026

Who is responsible

Atelier + Avenue is the controller for the AI Readiness Diagnostic. You can contact us at hello@atelierandavenue.com.

What we collect

  • Your name and work email address.
  • Your questionnaire answers and the profile, score, hurdle, action plan, reveal link and PDF link generated from those answers.
  • Technical request data needed to run and secure the service, such as request metadata and operational logs.

How we use it

  • To generate and deliver your AI-readiness profile, reveal page and PDF.
  • To create or update a Brevo contact record with your diagnostic links and profile metadata.
  • To operate, secure, troubleshoot and improve the diagnostic.
  • To follow up about the diagnostic or a working-session request connected to it.

Lawful bases

We rely on legitimate interests to provide the diagnostic you requested, keep the service secure and follow up about the result. If you ask us to discuss A+A services, we may also rely on taking steps at your request before entering a contract. We may rely on legal obligation where we need to keep or disclose information to comply with law.

This acknowledgement is not marketing consent. Marketing emails are handled separately and only sent where consent or another lawful route under UK GDPR and PECR applies.

Who receives it

We use Vercel to host and run the diagnostic, and Brevo to hold contact records and, once sender authentication is complete, deliver the requested profile email. We may also share information with professional advisers or service providers where necessary to run the business or comply with law.

International transfers

Our processors may process data outside the UK or EEA. Where that happens, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as adequacy arrangements, the UK International Data Transfer Agreement, EU Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent measures provided by those processors.

Retention

The reveal and PDF links use a sealed token that expires after 14 days. We do not run a separate questionnaire database in this app. Brevo contact and diagnostic metadata are kept for up to 24 months after the last meaningful interaction, unless you ask us to delete them sooner or we need to keep limited records for legal reasons. Operational logs are kept for short security and troubleshooting periods by our service providers.

Your rights

You can ask to access, correct, delete or restrict your personal data, receive a portable copy, object to processing based on legitimate interests, and withdraw marketing consent where consent is used.

You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint.

Automated profiling

The diagnostic automatically turns your answers into an indicative business profile and action plan. It does not make decisions about you with legal or similarly significant effects.

If you do not provide the data

Name, work email, questionnaire answers and this privacy acknowledgement are required to generate and deliver the profile. Without them, we cannot provide the reveal or PDF.
